Information of Parameters

1Smoking

Smoking is a harmful habit where individuals inhale tobacco or other substances, causing health risks like lung cancer, heart disease, and addiction due to nicotine. It's best avoided for better well-being.

2Yellow Fingers

Yellow finger symptoms typically involve discoloration, yellowing, or staining of the fingers due to tobacco use. This condition, known as "smoker's fingers," results from nicotine and tar exposure, impacting nail and skin color.

3Anxiety

Anxiety is a mental health condition characterized by excessive worry, fear, and restlessness. It often involves physical symptoms like rapid heartbeat and sweating, affecting daily life and well-being. Treatment options exist.

4Peer Pressure

Peer pressure is the influence exerted by one's social group to conform to their beliefs, behaviors, or choices. It can lead individuals to make decisions they might not have otherwise.

5Chronic Disease

Chronic disease refers to long-term health conditions that persist over time, often worsening gradually. These conditions, such as diabetes and heart disease, require ongoing management and can significantly impact one's quality of life.

6Fatigue

Fatigue is a state of extreme tiredness and lack of energy, both physical and mental. It can result from various factors, including overexertion, sleep deprivation, medical conditions, or stress, affecting daily functioning.

7Allergy

An allergy is an adverse immune system reaction to typically harmless substances (allergens) such as pollen, food, or pet dander. Symptoms range from mild to severe, including sneezing, itching, and anaphylaxis.

8Wheezing

Wheezing is a high-pitched, whistling sound produced during breathing, often due to narrowed airways. It can indicate respiratory issues like asthma, allergies, or infections, requiring medical evaluation and treatment.

9Alcohol Consumption

Alcohol consuming is the act of ingesting beverages containing ethanol, leading to altered mental and physical states. Excessive or irresponsible consumption can result in health problems, addiction, and social issues.

10Coughing

Coughing is a reflex action that clears the airway of irritants or mucus. It involves forceful expulsion of air from the lungs, often due to infections, allergies, or respiratory conditions.

11Shortness of Breath

Shortness of breath, or dyspnea, is a distressing sensation of difficulty breathing. It can be caused by various medical conditions, such as lung disease, heart problems, or anxiety, often requiring medical evaluation.

12Swallowing Difficulty

Swallowing difficulty, or dysphagia, is a condition where individuals struggle to swallow food or liquids. It can stem from various causes, including neurological issues, structural problems, or muscular weaknesses.

13Chest Pain

Chest pain is a discomfort or sensation of pressure or squeezing in the chest area. It can result from various causes, including heart issues, lung problems, or muscle strains, and requires prompt medical evaluation.

14Lung Cancer

Lung cancer is a malignant disease that originates in the lung tissues. It often results from long-term exposure to tobacco smoke and can spread, leading to severe health complications and mortality.
